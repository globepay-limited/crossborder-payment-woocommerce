The GlobePay Payments plugin allows you to accept WeChat Pay, Alipay and UnionPay via GlobePay payment gateway on your WordPress site easily.

== Description ==

Allow Canadian merchants to easily accept WeChat Pay, Alipay and UnionPay for their E-commerce Platform with GlobePay Payment Gateway . Users pay in Chinese RMB and merchants receive Canadian dollars ($CAD).

= Why choose GlobePay? =
GlobePay is a UK Payment Service Provider authorized by Alipay, WeChat Pay, and UnionPay that specializes in cross-border POS & mobile payment technology.

We are dedicated to providing a safe and convenient RMB cross-border payment platform for Chinese shoppers and Canadian businesses.

We make integrating Alipay, WeChat Pay and UnionPay for Canadian merchants easy and help enhance their share of the growing local Chinese market.

We offer solutions for businesses to drive commerce growth in a variety of industries ranging from Retail, Supermarkets, e-Commerce, Hotel, Food & Beverage, Transportation, Entertainment to Education.

More information is on [GlobePay Website](https://www.globepay.co) and [contact us](https://www.globepay.co/apply) to get your account ready.


== Features and Benefits ==
* One plugin integrates WeChat Pay, Alipay and UnionPay. Merchants can enable WeChat Pay, Alipay and UnionPay or all of them.
* Support pay from scanning QR Code on PC.
* Accept showing QR Code in GlobePay page or Local page.
* Support native pay from WeChat, Alipay and UnionPay Application on mobile (without extract QR Code).
* Accepts full and partial refunds.
* Customers pay with RMB and merchants receive CAD.
* Currency accepts CAD & CNY.
* Deposit to Canadian bank account directly.




== Installation ==

* In Wordpress -> Plugins menu, click “Add New”.  In the search field, search by “GlobePay - WeChat Pay, Alipay & UnionPay for WooCommerce”.
* Install and activate the plugin.
* Go to GlobePay' Website and [apply a merchant account](https://www.globepay.co/apply).
* After receive account information, you should enter Partner Code and Gateway Credential.
* Enable WeChat Pay, Alipay and UnionPay.

== Frequently Asked Questions ==

= The order showing on WooCommerce is not paid, but the customer has already paid with WeChat Pay, Alipay or UnionPay. Where should I check the order status? =
1. Login the [GlobePay BackOffice](https://pay.globepay.co) with your Login ID and password.
2. In WooCommerce Order Details, find the GlobePay_order_id and copy this number.
3. In GlobePay BackOffice, search the order_id you found in WooCommerce Order Details and check the order status.
4. If Pay Success in GlobePay BackOffice, change the order status in WooCommerce.

If you need more help, contact us 1-855-937-7888


= Does this require an SSL certificate? =

Yes! an SSL certificate must be installed on your site to use GlobePay (WeChat Pay, Alipay and UnionPay) which make sure payment complete under secure circumstance.
